Jawline Discrepancy Argument

One common argument suggesting that Bruce Wayne and Batman are not the same person focuses on physical appearance, particularly the jawline. Some observers claim that Batman’s mask and shadowed features make his jaw appear broader and more angular than Bruce Wayne’s softer public image. However, this difference is largely the result of lighting, facial expression, and the design of the cowl, which reshapes how his face is perceived. In reality, Batman deliberately uses posture, voice modulation, and concealment to create a distinct presence, reinforcing the illusion that Bruce Wayne and Batman are two separate individuals.
